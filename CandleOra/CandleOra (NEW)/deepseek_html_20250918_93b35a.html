Candle Fixes

For every candle question, there's a CandleOra solution — your trusted guide to keep the glow alive.

Candle Stops Burning in the Middle

Cause: Wick might be buried in wax or too short.
  1. Extinguish the candle and let it cool completely.
  2. Use a spoon or wick tool to gently remove wax around the wick.
  3. Trim the wick to about ½ inch and relight.
Watch Video Tutorial

Uneven Burning or Tunneling

Cause: Wick not centered or not burning long enough on first use.
  1. On the next burn, allow the wax to melt all the way to the edges.
  2. If tunneling is already there, use a foil wrap around the candle's rim to help even it out.
Watch Video Tutorial

Low Flame or Weak Scent Throw

Cause: Wick trimmed too short or fragrance not dispersing.
  1. Gently pour out a little melted wax or scoop it with a spoon.
  2. Relight to let the wick breathe and flame grow.

Frequently Asked Questions

What makes CandleOra candles special?
Our candles are handmade with love, tested for quality and burn performance, and designed to enhance your mood, freshen your space, and create a divine, calming ambiance.
Do CandleOra candles have scents?
Yes! Each candle is infused with carefully selected fragrances that match its category—whether it's Peace, Relax, Sleep, Fun, Love, Nature, or more.
How long do your candles burn?
We test each candle for optimal burn time. Depending on the size and design, our candles can burn anywhere from 20 to 60 hours without losing fragrance or quality.
Are your candles safe for indoor use?
Yes! Our candles are made with safe, high-quality wax and wicks. However, we always recommend placing them on a heat-resistant surface and never leaving them unattended while lit.
